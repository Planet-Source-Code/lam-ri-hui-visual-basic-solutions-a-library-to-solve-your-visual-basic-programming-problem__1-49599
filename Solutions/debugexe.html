How to Debug a Compiled Executable

In the IDE, you often use "Debug.Print ..." to verify or watch anything, but in the compiled Exe/Dll? The following simple code will do it (assumed you have installed the windows clipboard viewer):

Sub DebugClipboard(ByVal Text As Variant)
Dim dummy$
With Clipboard
  dummy = .GetText
  .Clear
  dummy = dummy & vbCrLf & Trim$(Text)
  .SetText dummy
End With
End Sub
